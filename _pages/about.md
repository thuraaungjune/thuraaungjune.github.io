I work on low-resource **Southeast Asian NLP**, focusing on Burmese. My research spans multilingual multimodal LLMs, Burmese NLP benchmark creation, dataset building and evaluation, speech and text processing, and methods for improving generalization when data is scarce.

I am currently studying software engineering with a specialization in artificial intelligence at King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Ladkrabang. My recent work includes research with AI Singapore and VISTEC on Southeast Asian language evaluation, and machine learning work at Looloo Technology on Thai speech and text systems.

## Selected highlights

- **Full Scholarship**: Child's Dream Foundation Scholarship covering tuition and living expenses.
- **Hackathon Winner**: Season 2 Championship Award for the 42 Bangkok x CDG Group Hackathon.
- **Best Paper Award**: 20th IEEE International Joint Symposium on Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing.

## Research focus

- Low-resource Burmese NLP and speech processing
- Multilingual and multimodal LLM evaluation
- Dataset creation for under-resourced Southeast Asian languages
- Robust methods for text classification, NER, OCR, ASR, and TTS
